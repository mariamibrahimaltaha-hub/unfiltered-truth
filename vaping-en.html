Myths vs Facts

What They Say… vs The Truth

Much of the information circulating about vaping is misleading. Discover the truth for yourself.

🤔
"It's just water vapor — it can't be harmful"
+
❌ Completely False

The vapor contains fine toxic particles that go directly to your lungs. These include nicotine, heavy metals like lead and nickel, and fatty particles that cause EVALI — a potentially fatal lung disease.

🤔
"Vaping helps you quit smoking"
+
❌ Not Scientifically Proven

There is insufficient scientific evidence that vaping is an effective quitting method. Most people who use it for that purpose end up dependent on both. The WHO does not recommend it as a treatment.

🤔
"No tar means no addiction"
+
❌ Common Misconception

Tar is not what causes addiction — nicotine is. Many vape devices contain up to 3× the nicotine concentration of regular cigarettes. Addiction happens faster and goes deeper.

🤔
"Nicotine-free vapes are completely safe"
+
❌ Not True

Even "nicotine-free" vape liquids contain harmful chemicals like acetaldehyde, formaldehyde, and glycols. These irritate the respiratory system and damage the lungs over time.

🤔
"The vapor doesn't harm people nearby"
+
❌ Secondhand Exposure Is Real

The aerosol from vaping contains nicotine and toxic particles that harm bystanders — especially children and pregnant women. The air doesn't clear immediately; particles remain suspended.

Chemicals Entering Your Body

Every puff delivers a cocktail of substances never meant to be inhaled.

⚗️
Nicotine
Highly addictive — affects the brain, heart, and blood pressure
☣️
Formaldehyde
A known carcinogen used in embalming fluid — yes, found in vapes too
🔋
Nickel & Lead
Heavy metals that leach from heating coils and accumulate in your lungs
🧪
Acetaldehyde
Classified as carcinogenic — damages the liver and respiratory tract
🌡️
Propylene Glycol
Irritates airways when heated, causing chronic cough and inflammation
💀
Diacetyl
Causes "popcorn lung" — permanent scarring with no known cure
Impact On Your Body

How Vaping Damages You

The damage doesn't stop at the lungs — every organ feels the effects.

🫁
Lungs
Acute inflammation and scarring, reduced oxygen capacity, and risk of EVALI — a deadly disease that has killed hundreds of young people.
🧠
Brain
Nicotine rewires neural circuits, impairs focus and memory, and increases the risk of anxiety and depression in young users.
❤️
Heart
Raises heart rate and blood pressure, constricts blood vessels — increasing the risk of cardiac events even in young people.
🦷
Mouth & Throat
Chronic dry mouth, gum inflammation, tooth discoloration, and throat sores — damaging your appearance before your health.
🧬
DNA
Studies show vape compounds cause DNA mutations linked to cancer — damage that may not appear for years.
😴
Sleep & Energy
Nicotine disrupts sleep cycles, causes chronic fatigue, and lowers energy levels and academic concentration.
